Chairs are made from different materials that may vary from wood to metal to synthetic material such as plastic, and they may be padded or upholstered in various colors and fabrics, either just on the seat, as with some dining room chairs or on the entire chair. Chairs are commonly seen and used in homes, whether it may be on living rooms, dining rooms, and dens, or in schools and offices, and in various other workplaces (Wikipedia, 2017).
